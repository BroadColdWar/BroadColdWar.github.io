IB History Analysis

This page is for the required analysis part of the assignment. It should sound like our own argument, not just copied facts.

IB Theme: Cold War

The Berlin Wall shows how the Cold War was fought through pressure, propaganda, military threats, and ideological competition without direct war between the U.S. and USSR.

IB Theme: Authoritarian States

East Germany used border control, surveillance, and police power to stop citizens from leaving. That connects to authoritarian methods of control.

Specific Class Connection

This can connect to containment, the Berlin Blockade, the Cuban Missile Crisis, or Soviet control over Eastern Europe after World War II.

Thesis Placeholder

The Berlin Wall mattered because it turned an invisible ideological conflict into something physical: a wall that separated families, governments, and two competing visions of the world.
