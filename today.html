Why It Matters Today

The Wall fell in 1989, but the questions it raised are still around: borders, migration, surveillance, propaganda, and whether governments should be able to trap their own citizens.

Borders

Modern border debates still involve security, identity, and who gets to move freely.

Surveillance

The East German state relied on monitoring and fear. Today, technology makes that even more powerful.

Propaganda

Both sides used Berlin as proof that their system was better. That type of messaging still exists online.

Memory

The Wall is a reminder that political systems affect normal people's daily lives, not just leaders.
