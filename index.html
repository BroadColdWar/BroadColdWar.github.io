The Berlin Wall

From 1961 to 1989, the Berlin Wall divided a city and became one of the most recognizable symbols of the Cold War. This website explains what it was, why it mattered, and how it connects to larger themes like alliances, nationalism, authoritarian control, and modern border politics.
